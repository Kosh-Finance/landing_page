Questions

Everything you want to know

Kosh is a unified finance platform that brings remittances, payroll, savings circles, credit scoring, a spending card, and treasury management into one app. Instead of using 7 different tools that each demand your identity and expose your data, Kosh lets you verify once and do everything from one place — with zero-knowledge privacy protecting every transaction.

Crypto finance is fragmented. You use Wise to send money, a separate tool for payroll, WhatsApp for savings circles, nothing for credit scoring, Gnosis Safe for treasury, and a CEX-linked card for spending. Each app requires separate KYC, exposes different data publicly, and none of them share identity or reputation.

Kosh eliminates this fragmentation tax by unifying everything under one ZK identity. Verify once, use everywhere, build credit from day one.

A ROSCA (Rotating Savings and Credit Association) is a savings circle where a group of people contribute monthly and each member takes turns receiving the full pool. They're called chit funds in India, stokvels in South Africa, tandas in Latin America. Billions of people use them worldwide.

Kosh's ZK-ROSCA is the world's first privacy-preserving on-chain savings circle. Contributions are anonymous, payouts are determined by a fair bid-based mechanism, and defaulters can be identified without compromising honest members' privacy. Every completed circle builds your ZK Credit Score.

The Kosh Card is a virtual (and eventually physical) spending card that lets you use your crypto balance — stablecoins, savings circle payouts, payroll earnings — at any merchant that accepts Visa or Mastercard.

Unlike existing crypto cards that require you to off-ramp through an exchange and link your entire wallet history, the Kosh Card draws from your private shielded balance on Midnight. The merchant gets paid in fiat. You spend in crypto. Your on-chain financial activity stays completely private.

Kosh is built natively on Midnight Network, which provides zero-knowledge privacy at the protocol level. Through LayerZero integration, Kosh connects to 50+ blockchains including Ethereum, Solana, Arbitrum, Base, Polygon, Avalanche, and more.

You can send from any supported chain and receive on any other — privately. Cardano serves as the settlement layer, anchoring every ZK proof to one of the most decentralized L1s in crypto.

Every financial action on Kosh — sending remittances, receiving payroll, completing savings circles — feeds a private credit score computed entirely in zero-knowledge. You can prove "my score is above 700" to a lending protocol without revealing the actual number, your income, or your transaction history.

It's the first credit scoring system where informal financial activity — the kind that billions of unbanked people conduct every day — builds formal, provable credit.
